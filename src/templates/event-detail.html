ABOUT THE ARTIST

Actor, writer and producer David Koechner is an American comedy heavyweight. He is best known for his roles as Todd Packer on "The Office" and Champ Kind from "Anchorman" and "Anchorman 2: The Legend Continues."

Koechner received rave reviews for his role in the dark, twisted and film festival award-winning thriller "Cheap Thrills." He stars in the CBS sitcom "Superior Donuts" and brings his unique brand of improvised comedy to stages across the country.
