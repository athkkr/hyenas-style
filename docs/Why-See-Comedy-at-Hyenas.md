FAQ: 

Do I have to order two items?
Yes! There’s a two-item minimum per person—food, drinks, or a mix of both. It keeps the good times rolling!

What’s the age requirement?
Fort Worth & Dallas: 18+ to enter.
‍Albuquerque: 21+ to enter.
No exceptions—make sure everyone in your group meets the requirement!


Do I need an ID?
Yes, every guest needs a valid ID—no matter how young or old you look.

Are ticket prices the same for every show?
General admission prices apply unless it’s a special event—those may have different pricing. Check before booking!

When should I arrive?
To keep your reservation and get the best seats, arrive at least 30 minutes before showtime.

How can I get free tickets?
Subscribe to our newsletter, and you might just score free tickets to select shows!
